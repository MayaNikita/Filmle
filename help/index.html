How to play

Your goal is to guess the movie or show with as few guesses as possible.

The poster will start off very blurry and grayscaled but will get more visible with each wrong guess.

If any information about the movie or show matches with the one you guessed, it will get filled out. For information that consists of a number (Year of release, Runtime & Rating) it will show you if your guess is too high or too low.
